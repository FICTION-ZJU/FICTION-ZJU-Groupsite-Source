Our paper has been accepted by the OOPSLA '26.

<!--more-->

Our paper titled "[A Tale of 1001 LoC: Potential Runtime Error-Guided Specification Synthesis for Verifying Large-Scale Programs](/publication/wang-OOPSLA2026/)" by {{% mention "Zhongyi Wang" %}}, {{% mention "Tengjie Lin" %}}, {{% mention "Mingshuai Chen" %}}, Haokun Li (Fermat Labs, Huawei Inc.), {{% mention "Mingqi Yang" %}}, Xiao Yi (Fermat Labs, Huawei Inc.), Shengchao Qin (Fermat Labs, Huawei Inc.), Yixing Luo, Xiaofeng Li, Bin Gu, Liqiang Lu, and Jianwei Yin has been accepted for publication at OOPSLA '26. This article presents Preguss -- a modular, fine-grained framework for automating the generation and refinement of formal specifications, especially for verifying real-world programs with over a thousand LoC.
