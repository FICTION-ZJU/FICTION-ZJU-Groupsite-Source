Our paper has been accepted by the OOPSLA '26.

<!--more-->

Our paper titled "[A Tale of 1001 LoC: Potential Runtime Error-Guided Specification Synthesis for Verifying Large-Scale Programs](/publication/zhan-fac2025/)" by {{% mention "Zhongyi Wang" %}}, {{% mention "Tengjie Lin" %}}, {{% mention "Mingshuai Chen" %}}, {{% mention "Haokun Li" %}}, {{% mention "Mingqi Yang" %}}, {{% mention "Xiao Yi" %}}, {{% mention "Shengchao Qin" %}}, {{% mention "Yixing Luo" %}}, {{% mention "Xiaofeng Li" %}}, {{% mention "Bin Gu" %}}, {{% mention "Liqiang Lu" %}}, and {{% mention "Jianwei Yin" %}} has been accepted for publication at OOPSLA '26. This article presents Preguss -- a modular, fine-grained framework for automating the generation and refinement of formal specifications, especially for verifying real-world programs with over a thousand LoC.
