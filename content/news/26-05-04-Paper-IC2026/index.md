Our paper has been accepted by Information and Computation.

<!--more-->

Our paper titled "[On Termination of Polynomial Programs with Equality Conditions](/publication/li-mpp2026/)" by Yangjia Li, {{% mention "Mingshuai Chen" %}}, Liangran Zhao, Naijun Zhan, Hui Lu, Guohua Wu, and Joost-Pieter Katoen has been accepted for publication at Information and Computation. This article shows that the set of non-terminating inputs of multi-path polynomial programs with equality conditions is algorithmically computable, which in turn yields the decidability of its termination on a given input.
