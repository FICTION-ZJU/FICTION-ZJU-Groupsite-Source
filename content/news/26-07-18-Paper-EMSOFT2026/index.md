Our paper titled "[Tolerant Barrier Certificates for Stochastic Systems](/publication/feng-tcad2026/)" by Shenghua Feng (ISCAS), Han Su (NII), Hao Wu (ISCAS), Jie An (ISCAS), {{% mention "Mingshuai Chen" %}}, and Naijun Zhan (PKU) has been accepted for publication at EMSOFT 2026, in Barcelona, Spain, as part of the IEEE/ACM Embedded Systems Week (ESWEEK). The paper is to be published in IEEE Transactions on Computer-Aided Design of Integrated Circuits and Systems (TCAD). This paper presents tolerant barrier certificates as a novel formalism to witness tolerant specifications of stochastic systems, where unsafe behavior is allowed as long as the total unsafe exposure remains below a prescribed budget with high probability.
